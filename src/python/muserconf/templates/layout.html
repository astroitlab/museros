Mingantu Ultrawide SpEctral Radioheliograph (MUSER)

Mingantu Ultrawide SpEctral Radioheliograph (MUSER) is a synthetic aperture radio interferometer. MUSER consists of total 100 radio antennas spirally distributed at Mingantu town, Inner Mongolia of China. The RF signal of MUSER in 0.4 - 15 GHz is divided into 0.4 - 2 GHz (subarray MUSER-I), and 2 - 15 GHz (subarray MUSER-II) bands. MUSER-I in 400 MHz - 2 GHz with 40 antennas of 4.5 meters, and MUSER-II in frequency range of 2 - 15 GHz with 60 antennas of 2 meters.
